## 🎮 **THE EXPERIENCE**

### **Entry Sequence (0-3 seconds)**
When visitors arrive, they experience:
- 🔹 **Boot Screen**: "Initializing Shivam AI System..."
- 🔹 **Progress Animation**: Sci-fi loading bar with glowing effects
- 🔹 **System Messages**: Rotating tech messages building anticipation
- 🔹 **Cinematic Reveal**: Smooth transition into the 3D world

### **Main Environment**
Once loaded, users enter a **futuristic AI workspace** featuring:

#### **🧊 Floating Project Cubes**
- 3 rotating cubes representing your projects
- Each cube glows and reacts to hover
- Click to expand project details
- Positioned in a semi-circle arrangement

#### **🌐 Neural Sphere (Skills Hub)**
- Central glowing sphere with orbiting skill nodes
- Skills: Python, ML, YOLO, OpenCV, Git, Web
- Connected by neural network lines
- Rotates slowly with floating animation

#### **📜 Holographic Display (About)**
- Transparent hologram screen showing your profile
- Displays: Name, College, CGPA
- Scan line effects for authentic hologram feel
- Floats gently in the center area

#### **🏆 Achievement Pods**
- 4 trophy pods floating around the space
- Each represents an achievement
- Golden glow on hover
- Labels show achievement names

#### **⚡ Environmental Features**
- **Grid Floor**: Neon cyberpunk grid pattern
- **Star Field**: Distant stars for depth
- **Ambient Particles**: 500+ floating data particles
- **Neural Network Background**: Animated connections
- **Dynamic Lighting**: Multiple colored point lights
